Introducing the album


TOMORROW X TOGETHER delivers their story through ‘The Dream Chapter’. As they transition from childhood to adolescence, the boys have gathered together under ONE DREAM. For TOMORROW X TOGETHER, star is another name for dream: the emotions swelling up inside as you look up at the starry night sky, the growing excitement about tomorrow – chasing the stars mean you are remembering your childhood dreams. ‘The Dream Chapter’ will unfold the stories of what they encountered as they grew.

A compilation of 5 tracks in total, the album sings out TXT’s “happiness and excitement of meeting that special someone.”
