Introducing the album

The title track, "I Don't Want to Cry," which opens the "age of sadness" where a boy meets and overcomes sadness! Their namesake 2017 "SEVENTEEN" first album [Al1] that opens this year contains the new charm of "SEVENTEEN" that exceeds expectations. [Al1] contains another step in the boy's growth shown in the album released by Seventeen over the past two years. If the songs of "SEVENTEEN," which lead to "Adore U," "Manse," "Pretty U," "Very NICE" and "Boom Boom," are about a boy getting to know the feelings of love, "I Don't Want to Cry" is a song about the first deep moment of sadness he felt in his life. When the moment came when he might lose someone, who was like the boy's whole life, he expressed his despair and pure heart that he never wanted to lose it with the words "I don't want to cry." Maintaining the pure emotions of the boy that "SEVENTEEN" has shown so far, but showing a deeper appearance. In order to show such deep emotions, "I Don't Want to Cry" also attempted EDM for the first time as "SEVENTEEN". As the synthesizer containing lyrical sensibility and the violent beat intersect, it captures the heart of "SEVENTEEN," who feels like tears will flow, but does not want to cry.
