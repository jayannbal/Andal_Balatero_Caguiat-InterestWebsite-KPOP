Introducing the album


Still Dreaming is the first Japanese studio album by TOMORROW X TOGETHER. It was released on January 20, 2021 with "Blue Hour (Japanese Ver.)" serving as the album's title track.

The ten-track album comprises Japanese versions of six of the group's Korean songs; two new instrumental tracks; a new original Japanese song, "Force"; and their previous Japanese single, "Everlasting Shine". Recorded in Japanese and English, Still Dreaming is a J-pop album that borrows numerous musical elements including disco, pop rock, and synth-pop.
