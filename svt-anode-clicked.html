Introducing the album


SEVENTEEN is a perfect and mature music producer...The 3rd studio album [An Ode] will be released! Seventeen's third full-length album [An Ode] is an extension of the digital single "HIT," which heralded a challenge by dreaming of expanding into a new world. In response, just as the speaker metaphorically expresses the message through "Ode (Poetry)," Seventeen also melted the message they wanted to convey in various ways and expressed the special meaning of "SEVENTEEN's melody."

In addition, SEVENTEEN faces the feeling of "fear" that anyone can feel at least once in their lives as one of the emotions they feel through the process of creation, giving them a deeper insight into themselves and a more mature complete music through [An Ode].
