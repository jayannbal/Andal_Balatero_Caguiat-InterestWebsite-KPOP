Introducing the album

"Happy Ending" is the first Japanese single by SEVENTEEN. It was released on May 29, 2019, with "Happy Ending" serving as the title track.

The overall theme of SEVENTEEN's "Happy Ending" is about love, relationships, and wanting to protect the one that you care about. The lyrics describe the feeling of being in a movie-like romance where time stops when you first see your significant other's smile, and you become deeply attracted to them. The song's verses highlight the importance of protecting and comforting your loved one during difficult times, while the pre-chorus and chorus celebrate the happiness and love in a relationship. The song's bridge describes a perfect love story where the two people involved are happy and fully committed to each other. The final lines of the song ("So for now") suggest a hopeful and optimistic outlook on life and love, and that even when the movie ends, there is still more to come. Overall, "Happy Ending" is a celebratory song about love, commitment, and the joys of being in a fulfilling relationship with someone you care about deeply.
