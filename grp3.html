Blackpink

BLACKPINK, a global K-pop phenomenon, has made a significant impact on the music industry, breaking down barriers and redefining what it means to be a girl group. The band is composed of four fearless members—JISOO, JENNIE, ROSÉ, and LISA—who push the boundaries of what is possible. Beyond musicians, they are also style icons and cultural influencers.

Blackpink

The meaning of BLACKPINK aims to contradict the common perception of the color pink. Pink is commonly used to portray prettiness, but BLACKPINK actually means to say that 'Pretty isn’t everything.' It also symbolizes that they are a team that encompasses not only beauty, but also great talent.

The group debuted on August 8 with their first digital single album, "Square One". The single album has two songs, "Whistle" and "Boombayah".

It charted at number one and two on the Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart, making Blackpink the fastest act to achieve such a feat and the third Korean artist to hold the top two positions simultaneously. They won first place on Inkigayo thirteen days after their debut, breaking the record for the shortest time for a girl group to win on a music program after debut.

"BLINK"

Blackpink revealed the name of their fan club "Blink", a portmanteau of "black" and "pink."
