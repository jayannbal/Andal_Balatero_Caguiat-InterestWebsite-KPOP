Tomorrow X Together

TOMORROW X TOGETHER, read as ‘tomorrow by together’, also known as TXT, is a bright and youthful boyband aiming to become the next global breakthrough artist. The band is composed of five members, SOOBIN, YEONJUN, BEOMGYU, TAEHYUN, HUENINGKAI, where each member is different from one another, shining in their own way to create synergy.

Group name

Their name refers to five individuals who "come together under one dream in hopes of building a better tomorrow

They debuted on March 4, 2019, with the extended play (EP) The Dream Chapter: Star.

Following the release of the EP, the music video of the album's lead single, "Crown" garnered 14.5 million views on YouTube

Fandom name

Their fandom name is called MOA (Moments Of Alwaysness) which refers to the moments that TXT and the fans always share.
