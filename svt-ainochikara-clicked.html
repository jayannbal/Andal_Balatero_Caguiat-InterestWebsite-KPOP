Introducing the album

The song "あいのちから (Power of Love / Ainochikara)" by SEVENTEEN (세븐틴) revolves around the theme of the power of love and the resilience it brings to individuals. The lyrics portray a story set on a snowy day, highlighting the challenges faced by the characters and how love empowers them to overcome adversity.

The chorus, led by Jeonghan and Hoshi, speaks of the anticipation of knowing what awaits once the cold winter passes. The contrasting images of freezing and blooming hearts indicate that even in the harshest of times, love's warmth can allow individuals to grow stronger. The post-chorus, performed by Vernon and Jun, emphasizes the accumulation of hearts that have endured and experienced hardships, signifying the collective strength that love can bring when united. Overall, "あいのちから (Power of Love / Ainochikara)" conveys the message that love has the ability to overcome hardships, heal wounds, and empower individuals to navigate through life's challenges with resilience and strength.
