Introducing the album


'Do It Like That' is a summer tempo dance track with impressive lyrics and melodies expressing the ecstatic moment of falling in love. The catchy chorus is attractive enough for anyone to sing along from the first note, while the lyrics exuding a happy mood, excited by the fever of love, invigorate the entire song.

The song "Do It Like That" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER & Jonas Brothers is about being captivated by someone who has a magnetic and mesmerizing effect on the narrator. The lyrics express a sense of awe and disbelief at how this person can effortlessly capture the attention and admiration of everyone around them.
