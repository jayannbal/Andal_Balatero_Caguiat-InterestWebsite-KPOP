Introducing the album

'Back for More (with Anitta)' is a disco genre song with a groovy bassline and whistle sound, with the colors of trendy Latin pop. It a song that says that there are magical, miraculous moments even in everyday life, and for me, reuniting with 'you' will be that moment. Unlike Tomorrow by Together's previous disco songs, which had a bright and cheerful atmosphere, this song stands out with its sexy charm.
The song "Back for More" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER & Anitta seems to revolve around the theme of a romantic relationship that is filled with uncertainty and the fear of losing each other. The lyrics describe a strong connection between two individuals, where being together feels different and makes sense. However, there is also an acknowledgment that there might be doubts and insecurities within the relationship. Throughout the song, there is a sense of longing for the other person to stay and not walk away. The repetition of the line "I can see you comin' back for more, comin' back for more" implies that the narrator believes their partner will return despite any challenges they may face. It showcases their trust in the strength of their bond and the hope that their partner will realize they need each other.
