Introducing the album


"Pink Venom" is the second digital single by BLACKPINK. It was released on August 19, 2022. It serves as the pre-release single and the first track of BLACKPINK's second Korean studio album, Born Pink.

The song "Pink Venom" is about empowerment. The lyrics discuss the power of Blackpink, a South Korean girl group, and how they are not afraid to break the rules and take risks in order to achieve their goals. The members present a strong persona, highlighting their courage and fearlessness, with themes such as approachability, confidence, and rebelliousness. They emphasize the notion of having power within yourself to create change, and highlight the importance of female empowerment.
