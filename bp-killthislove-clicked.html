Introducing the album

Kill This Love is the second Korean mini-album by BLACKPINK. It was released on April 5, 2019 with "Kill This Love" serving as the album's title track and "Don't Know What To Do" as a sub-title track.

"Kill This Love" by BLACKPINK is a song about ending toxic, unhealthy relationships. It touches on the themes of love and abandonment, as well as the inner conflict that arises when someone is stuck in an emotionally draining and painful romantic relationship. The song speaks to the complexity of emotions surrounding love and relationships, emphasizing that sometimes the best thing for a person to do is to move on, and “kill” the love in order to protect themselves. The song also speaks to the idea of loving too much, and being taken advantage of in that regard, as the lyrics say, “His love is my favorite but you plus me sadly can be dangerous”. This idea of loving too much is juxtaposed by the chorus of “Let’s Kill This Love”, ultimately reflecting the need to come to terms with the idea of cutting ties in order to save oneself.
