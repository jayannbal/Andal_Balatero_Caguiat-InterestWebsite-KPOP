Introducing the album

"Ice Cream" is a collaboration between BLACKPINK and Selena Gomez. It was released on August 28, 2020. It serves as the second pre-release single and the second track of BLACKPINK's first Korean studio album, The Album.

The song "Ice Cream" by BLACKPINK and Selena Gomez is an upbeat, catchy anthem about a relationship that has progressed to a point of physical intimacy. Throughout the song, the two singers sing of all the things they enjoy about the other — including the delicious and alluring feeling they feel when they are together — while comparing the relationship to "ice cream".
