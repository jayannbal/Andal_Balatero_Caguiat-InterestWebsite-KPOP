Introducing the album

"Ice Cream" is a collaboration between BLACKPINK and Selena Gomez. It was released on August 28, 2020. It serves as the second pre-release single and the second track of BLACKPINK's first Korean studio album, The Album.

The song "Ice Cream" by BLACKPINK and Selena Gomez is an upbeat, catchy anthem about a relationship that has progressed to a point of physical intimacy. Throughout the song, the two singers sing of all the things they enjoy about the other — including the delicious and alluring feeling they feel when they are together — while comparing the relationship to "ice cream". They celebrate the thrill of being together and the physical attraction between them, calling themselves "ice cream" as a way to show the other their affection. The imagery of "sippin' like a Slurpee" and "catch me in the fridge" implies the cool, refreshing feeling of being in the other's arms and how the "coldest with the kiss" allows them to get out of the heat and into a blissful, romantic space. The song is a fun, light-hearted declaration of love.
