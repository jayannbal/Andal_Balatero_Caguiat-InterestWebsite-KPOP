Introducing the album


SWEET is the second Japanese studio album by TOMORROW X TOGETHER. It was released on July 5, 2023 with "Sugar Rush Ride (Japanese Ver.)" serving as the album's title track. The physical album comes in three versions: Standard Edition, Limited Edition A and Limited Edition B (CD+DVD).

The song "Sugar Rush Ride (Japanese Ver.)" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER explores themes of temptation, addiction, and the allure of indulgence. The lyrics depict a captivating relationship that is filled with sweet and enticing moments, but also hints at the darker aspects that come along with it.
