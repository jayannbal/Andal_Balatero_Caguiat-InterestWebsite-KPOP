Introducing the album

Seventeen opened the project with Wonwoo and Mingyu's digital single "Bittersweet" released on May 28. Wonwoo and Mingyu's Bittersweet, which solved the dilemma between love and friendship between the three men and women in mixed situations, showed the first form of love and depicted a story of courageously confessing the heart of "I" to "You," which made them realize the feelings of love through their second form of love, the 8th mini album [Your Choice].

Here, we talk about the third form of love with our 9th mini album [Attacca]. Various forms of love and feelings of "Jeongyeol" met to reveal deeper love emotions, further expanding the way Seventeen can show. The album name [Attacca] is an album that figuratively expresses Seventeen's enterprising love method by expressing the feelings of uncontrollable love toward the other person and the unstoppable passionate heart when the next movement continues at the end of one movement.
