Introducing the album

"SEVENTEEN's Yechan of Youth" 7th mini album! SEVENTEEN's 7th mini-album [Henggarae] contains a powerful energy to escape the feelings of "fear" that they faced on their third regular album [An Ode] and move to a new world together with "dream" and send a message of support to young people who are challenging for their dreams.

Through this album, rather than expressing the pain of young people who are struggling at a crossroads, Seventeen will first reach out to them with their own ideas, cheer them on, permeate them with passionate energy, and show a cheerful and unique charm. "Left & Right," the title song of the healing album of youth, which is captured in SEVENTEEN's own color, is a song that was reborn as the sound of 2020 by devising the hip-hop genre in the 2000s, and gives powerful energy to those who feel there is no way out, either left or right, positively shouting "Let's go out strong without fear."
