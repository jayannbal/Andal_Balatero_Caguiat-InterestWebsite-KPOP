Introducing the album

The song "24H" by SEVENTEEN is a love song describing the profound and unbreakable bond between two individuals that transcends time and distance. The lyrics suggest that the narrator is deeply in love with someone and is willing to spend every moment with them. They feel that being by their loved one's side is the most important thing in their life and they are perfect together. The verse also indicates that they both share an intimate relationship that is only between them and that they can escape from the world's troubles by being in each other's company.
The song also hints that time is not equal when it comes to love. Time is not enough to express their emotions because they wish to spend every moment with their loved one. They are willing to climb any mountain and face any hardship, just to be there for their loved one. The lyrics suggest that every second is precious, and they want to cherish every moment with that special someone. The chorus repeats the phrase "24 hours" throughout the song, signifying that their love is limitless and constant, regardless of the hour of the day.
