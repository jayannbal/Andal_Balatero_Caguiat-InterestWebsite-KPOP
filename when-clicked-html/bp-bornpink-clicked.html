Introducing the album


Born Pink (stylized in all uppercase and alternatively titled "BLΛƆKPIИK Born Pink") is the second Korean studio album by BLACKPINK. It was released on September 16, 2022 with "Shut Down" serving as the title track. "Pink Venom" was released as a pre-released single on August 19, 2022. The physical album comes in nine versions: Black, Pink, Gray, Kit, LP, and individual member digipack.

On August 24, YG Entertainment revealed that BLACKPINK are filming the music video for the title track for Born Pink in Gyeonggi province. They also added that the title track will shock the fans and that the music video will raise the status of K-pop quality to something that hasn't been seen before.[6]
