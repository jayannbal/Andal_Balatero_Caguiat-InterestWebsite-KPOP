Introducing the album

Seventeen, the "irreplaceable K-pop leader" who pre-released their first English single "Darl+ing" in April and delivered a warm message that we should become "one" and share our sincere feelings, has returned with their fourth full-length album [Face the Sun]. Seventeen told the story of their 13 fears through the trailer video "13 Inner Shadows" released by each member ahead of the release of their fourth full-length album [Face the Sun]. Seventeen, who has taken a step closer to fans as a more authentic artist by expressing different inner stories without addition or subtraction, chooses to burn all distrust toward them and become stronger beings and embark on a new journey through [Face the Sun] instead of being eaten up by fear.
The fourth full-length album [Face the Sun] is an album that honestly captures SEVENTEEN's ambition to become the only "sun" in the world. As revealed in the album name, Seventeen's journey to becoming the sun, even if the journey to becoming the best, implies a strong will to become the sun by looking forward without avoiding everything they face.
