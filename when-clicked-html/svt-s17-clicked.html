Introducing the album

The SECTOR of the album name [SECTOR 17] means "SECTOR," which refers to a high mountain over 8,000m above sea level. There are currently 16 seats on the planet. The 17th left is the 'unknown territory' that no one has found. Therefore, SEVENTEEN, who has reached [SECTOR 17], which can be called 17 seats, which is an unknown area, will reach the new summit. [SECTOR 17] is not a specific point such as a mountain peak, but a territory of Seventeen that will expand endlessly, and it tells the story of "TEAM SVT," which has traveled together there, becoming one true and unfolding our own world.

Seventeen does not stop at reaching its destination. They present a message that they will endlessly take on new challenges and continue their adventures with their fans, expressing their willingness to become the "most "HOT" artist in the world's K-pop scene." As such, the fourth full-length album Repackage [SECTOR 17] will deliver "SEVENTEEN reaching the highest point to overcome the sun" and "a new world completed by Seventeen by pioneering a place that no one else has ever reached."
