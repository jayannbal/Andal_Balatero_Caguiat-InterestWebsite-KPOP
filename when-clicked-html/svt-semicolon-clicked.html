Introducing the album

SEVENTEEN's special album "; [Semicolon]", "A Feast of Youth" Seventeen's album '; [Semicolon]' is an album that shares again the message of support for 'youth' talked about through its 7th mini album 'Henggarae' and sends a more mature positive message to the youth who run nonstop, 'Let's take a break and enjoy the feast of youth'.

For young people who are struggling in the present era, they cut off the sentences of "time to breathe" and "time to laugh and enjoy together" for a while, and compared it to "Semicolon," a punctuation code used to continue explaining, and "SEVENTEEN" living as contemporary youth, full of messages expressing their sympathy in the album to convey warm comfort, support, and hope that will caress the youth. The title song "HOME;RUN" is a song with a retro sound based on the swing genre, expressing the thrill of the expression "HOME RUN" with a cheerful and striking sound, and gives a powerful voice of support and comfort to young people with infinite growth.
