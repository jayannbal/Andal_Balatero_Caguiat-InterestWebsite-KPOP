Introducing the album

SEVENTEEN is back with a repackage album! This album was designed to express gratitude to fans for their enthusiastic love for the last "Pretty U." activity. It consists of a total of 15 songs, including five new songs and songs from the existing LOVE & LETTER, and you can feel the rich music color of SEVENTEEN.

The title song of this album, "Very NICE", is a song that expresses a man's heart toward his favorite reason with the word NICE, expressing the excitement and heartthrob of spending a nice day with the meaning of the word NICE with Seventeen's own cheerfulness. It will provide a refreshing and fresh image that visually expresses the feeling that "when I meet my favorite reason and my eyes, my heart is fluttering as if it burst." The performance of "Very NICE," which is more advanced from the performance of the title song "Mansae" of Seventeen's second mini album [BOYS BE], will show a more powerful and flashy group dance than "Mansae," and you can feel the lively charm of Seventeen.
