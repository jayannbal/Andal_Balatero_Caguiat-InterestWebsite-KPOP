Introducing the album


SEVENTEEN's mature musical performance! It shows their terrifying growth with bold changes.

The title song "Home" captures the desire to light up the darkness and make you feel comfortable, expresses the importance of your favorite partner with a fond emotion, and compares the desire to overcome any difficulties as a being who can lean on each other to "home" to make listeners feel comfortable with warm lyrics and delicate emotional lines.
