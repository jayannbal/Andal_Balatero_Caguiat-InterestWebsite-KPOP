Introducing the album

The song "ひとりじゃない (Not Alone / Hitorijanai)" by SEVENTEEN conveys a message of comfort, support, and the importance of having someone by your side. It is a song that spreads the message of finding solace and strength in the presence of others, reassures individuals that they are not alone in their struggles, and highlights the significance of having a supportive companion by one's side.

The pre-chorus reiterates the message that the narrator is not alone. There is warmth in their heart that remains, assuring them that as the night passes, they will eventually meet the person who has always been by their side. They express gratitude for always having that person with them and emphasize that tears are unnecessary because they have the support they need. The chorus emphasizes the phrase "ひとりじゃない" (hitorijanai), repeating it as a reaffirmation. The phrase, which means "not alone," serves as a reminder to the narrator and the listeners that they are not alone because they have someone by their side.
