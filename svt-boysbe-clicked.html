Introducing the album

Boys as boys. [SEVENTEEN 2nd Mini Album 'BOYS BE'] The rookie "SEVENTEEN," who received the most attention in the music industry in the first half of 2015, is back with their second mini-album [BOYS BE].

In addition, It contains "SEVENTEEN" as it is, which cannot be expressed in forced or standardized packaging, and "SEVENTEEN," which will continue toward perfection at the same time, within the title [BOYS BE]. Adding to the unique refreshing energy created when 13 boys are together and the infinite development potential felt through the music they make themselves, the second mini-album of "SEVENTEEN" (BOYS BE) was completed. The title song "Mansae" is a song completed by the lyrics and composition of "Woozi" and the composition and arrangement of powerful bass riffs and unique songs are impressive. With the vocal unit's outstanding singing ability, hip-hop unit's rap, and the performance unit's leader, Hoshi's choreography, it was completed as a song that felt the joy and power of 13 people at the same time.
