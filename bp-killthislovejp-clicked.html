Introducing the album

Kill This Love -JP Ver.- is the second Japanese studio album released by BLACKPINK. It was released on October 16, 2019. The album includes 10 tracks in total including both Japanese and Korean versions of the tracks in the Kill This Love mini-album.

"Kill This Love (Japan Version)" by BLACKPINK explores the theme of a toxic and destructive love relationship. "Kill This Love (Japan Version)" by BLACKPINK portrays a narrative of a love gone wrong, highlighting the destructive and harmful nature of the relationship. The song encourages listeners to recognize the toxicity in their own relationships and make the decision to end them for their own well-being. The lyrics depict a narrative where the speaker is trapped in a love that causes pain and suffering. The speaker acknowledges that they are unable to maintain their sanity in this love and mentions the high price they have paid for it.
