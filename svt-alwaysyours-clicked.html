Introducing the album


Always Yours (stylized in all caps) is the first Japanese best album by SEVENTEEN. It was released on August 23, 2023 with "Ima -Even If the World Ends Tomorrow-" serving as the album's title track.

In the verses, S.COUPS and HOSHI express their desire to protect something precious, symbolized by someone's smile. They reflect on their journey from ordinary days to becoming adults, acknowledging that it has been challenging but not regretful. Overall, "今 -明日 世界が終わっても- (Ima -Even if the world ends tomorrow-)" encourages listeners to treasure the fleeting moments and relationships they have in the present, expressing a desire to protect the happiness and smiles that bring meaning to their lives, even in the face of potential endings.
