Introducing the album


Re: BLACKPINK is a repackage of the debut Japanese mini-album BLACKPINK by BLACKPINK. It was released on March 28, 2018. The release comes in three versions: a CD edition, a CD+DVD edition, and a Playbutton version. The new repackage album includes the same contents as their Japan Debut Mini Album with the addition of ‘BLACKPINK PREMIUM DEBUT SHOWCASE’ stages, A-nation 2017, making movie, special interview, and photobook for DVD version.
