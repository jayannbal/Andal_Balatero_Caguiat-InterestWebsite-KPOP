Introducing the album


"Kiss and Make Up" is a collaboration song recorded by British singer-songwriter Dua Lipa and BLACKPINK. It was released on October 19, 2018, and it is a song in the album Dua Lipa: Complete Edition. It is a bilingual dance track about trying to “Kiss and Make Up” before a relationship ends in a break up. This is the first time BLACKPINK has collaborated with an non-Korean artist. Built around the repetition of the titular phrase and thriving on the five women's addictive vocals, it features the four members of BLACKPINK ~ Jisoo, Rosé, Lisa, and Jennie -- as they weave Korean and English with Dua Lipa’s huskier tone.

It is the first time the two acts have worked together, and the first time BLACKPINK has been featured as a whole on another artist’s album.
