Introducing the album


Your Light is the first OST by TXT and the main theme song of the JTBC teen-drama Live On. It was released on November 24, 2020. Apart from the original version, there is a Japanese version and an instrumental version.

The song "Your Light" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER is an uplifting anthem that encourages listeners to embrace their unique qualities and follow their dreams. The lyrics convey a message of self-empowerment, encouraging individuals to shine brightly and express themselves without fear or hesitation.
