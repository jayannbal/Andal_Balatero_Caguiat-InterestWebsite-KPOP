Introducing the album


The ‘Chaos Chapter’ depicts a world in which dreams have been swept away to be replaced by the towering presence of reality. As the world he once knew crumbles away, the boy feels small, insignificant, empty, and frozen. But ‘you’ awaken the emotion of ‘love’ inside of him, and the boy now seeks to step out beyond the freeze. For ‘you,’ he wants to make a choice: to FIGHT against the world or to ESCAPE together with you.

The Chaos Chapter: FIGHT OR ESCAPE consists of eleven tracks total, eight of which were previously released through The Chaos Chapter: FREEZE. The repackaged album debuts three new tracks:
