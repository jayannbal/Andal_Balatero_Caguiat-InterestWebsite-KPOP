Introducing the album

SEVENTEEN has released its third mini album Seventeen 3rd Mini Album [Going Seventeen] and returns with brilliant passion. Consisting of eight new songs with the keyword "passion toward dreams."

The title song "Boom Boom" expresses the excitement of running for a goal through these dreams and passion and the pounding heart of seeing a favorite reason through the word "BOOM." It's a song that allows you to feel Seventeen's passionate energy to your heart's content, and you can easily hum it by being stuck in your mind, and you can feel the energy of Seventeen more powerful than before.

In addition, the members of "Going Seventeen" also participated in the title song, just like Seventeen, who made the song and choreography themselves as a "self-produced idol" since their debut. In particular, SEVENTEEN member Woozi and composer Bumzu and other members of SEVENTEEN will also participate in the album's track work to showcase SEVENTEEN's musical capabilities that are growing more than before.
