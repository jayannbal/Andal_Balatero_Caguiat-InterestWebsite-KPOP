Introducing the album

SEVENTEEN's comeback with musical growth is a release that melts the emotions of youth! Seventeen, which has firmly established itself as the epitome of "refreshing" by providing unrivaled performance and bright energy, finally made its debut in public on July 16 with another fifth mini album.

Seventeen's announcement in five months contains a moment that can be shown because it is Seventeen's most beautiful youth. Growth based on this is becoming SEVENTEEN's own identity. In response, Seventeen will present their dazzling and brilliant beauty with more mature refreshing beauty than before.

The title name of SEVENTEEN's fifth mini album means two things: "You make me feel good" and "You give me a good day," as well as expressing the happy moment as "DAY," which makes it even more special.
