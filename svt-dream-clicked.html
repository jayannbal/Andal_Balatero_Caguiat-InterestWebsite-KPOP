Introducing the album

Dream is the third Japanese EP by SEVENTEEN. It was released on November 9, 2022 with "DREAM" serving as the album's title track. "DREAM" by SEVENTEEN is a song that explores the themes of dreams, reality, and the pursuit of happiness. The lyrics suggest a desire to escape the monotony of everyday life and find solace in the world of dreams.

The pre-chorus reflects on the fleeting nature of time and poses the question of whether they are actively chasing their dreams or being pursued by them, emphasizing the uncertainty and transience of life. The chorus reaffirms the power of dreams, representing them as a meeting place where the members encounter someone special. The lyrics express the sentiment that this connection feels so real, like magic, and elicits overflowing emotions. They describe how everything, from time and air to temperature and the person they long for, becomes their whole existence. It signifies a profound connection and the culmination of their dreams.
