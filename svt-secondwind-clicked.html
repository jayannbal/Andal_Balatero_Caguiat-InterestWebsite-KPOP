Introducing the album

The name of the first single album "SECOND WIND" means "second breath" or "new vitality" that allows you to continue what has exhausted you. It means a time or change when you feel the "most thrilling moment" as the pain decreases after passing through the "dead point" where you feel physical pain due to intense exercise.

"SECOND WIND" is an album full of members' aspirations to breathe new breaths into the music industry through their second album, just as they breathe new breaths into your tired daily life. The title song "Feat. Lee Young-ji" was composed by SEVENTEEN members S.Coups and Hoshi in addition to music producers Woozi and BUMZU, and all members of Boo Seok-soon participated in the lyrics, adding to expectations. Rapper Lee Young-ji, who has become an icon of the MZ generation, quickly gained recognition with the title song, "Loving You Girl (Feat. Hkeem)" and "Bonfire," and rising pop star Peder Elias participated in the album to raise the level of completion.
