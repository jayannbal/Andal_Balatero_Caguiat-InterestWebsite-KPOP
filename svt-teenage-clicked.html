Introducing the album

"TEEN, AGE" simultaneously means "Teenage" (teenager), which can be said to represent Seventeen, and "The Age of Seventeen." As such, the second full-length album "TEEN, AGE" can be said to mean a lot to SEVENTEEN. In particular, "TEEN, AGE" is significant in that it marks the last and new beginning of "2017 SEVENTEEN PROJECT," which was conducted as a large project of SEVENTEEN during 2017.

This album contains the growth and new changes through various emotions that Seventeen has experienced since their debut in 2015 and the love they received from fans. The title song "Clap" compressively shows Seventeen's change and growth with a refreshing and powerful appearance at the same time. No matter how hard and painful it is, it contains the determination and healthy energy to break through the hearts of Seventeen and the fans who support Seventeen together. In addition, this can be said to be the "new world" of Seventeen in that it retains its unique charm while breaking the framework so far and creating a new appearance of Seventeen.
