Introducing the album


"How You Like That" is the first single album by BLACKPINK. The single and its music video was released on June 26, 2020 as the first pre-release single from the group's debut Korean studio album, The Album, which was released on October 2, 2020. Teddy co-wrote the track with Danny Chung, and its producers R. Tee and 24. It's the group's first release in South Korea after being on hiatus for one year and two months.

The song is described as "hip-hop influenced, classic BLACKPINK song with grand and intense production featuring explosive beat drops.
