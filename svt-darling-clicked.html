Introducing the album

The digital single "Darl+ing" is a word that calls for a loving and special being, and at the same time, by adding an addition symbol (+), it means "Let's meet a special you and I and become complete one as us." It contains a message that "it is important to be one" by sharing sincere feelings with loved ones, and a honest wish that everyone around the world with Seventeen would unite as "we" and become a complete being.
Along with this, "Darl+ing" also means that although there may be moments when they are fiercely opposed to each other in a relationship, their sincere hearts, who truly love and love each other, do not fundamentally change.
As SEVENTEEN hopes to always be one as "we" in any situation and melts their inner honest feelings in "Darl+ing," they will completely capture the hearts of listeners around the world with a more sincere attitude than ever before.
