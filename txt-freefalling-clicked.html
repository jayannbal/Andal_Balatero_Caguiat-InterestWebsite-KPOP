Introducing the album


"Free Falling" is a song by TOMORROW X TOGETHER. It premiered on Webtoon on September 18, 2022 and was officially released on September 19, 2022. It is the first soundtrack for the original story THE STAR SEEKERS.

"Free Falling" is an empowering and uplifting song that encourages listeners to face their fears, embrace freedom, and keep striving for greater heights. The lyrics convey a sense of fear and uncertainty that is eventually transformed into courage and liberation.It captures the transformative journey from fear and uncertainty to liberation and growth.
