Introducing the album

Seventeen, which announced the start of the 2021 project "Power of Love," talked about the various moments of love they can face in their lives through this project, predicting that they will fill 2021 with honest and colorful feelings of love, drawing a lot of attention and expectations from listeners around the world. Your Choice, the album name of the 8th mini album, means that your choice is more important than my choice. As a result, he redefined and expanded the mature appearance that only Seventeen can show by continuing the kind-hearted Seventeen's innocence of sharing the concerns of "I" and respecting the choice of "You."
The title song "Ready to Love" is an R&B-based pop genre song with an attractive sensuous synth base and striking beats, and you can find their strong charm from Seventeen's unique energetic and manic melodies. The lyrics depicting the story of "I," who fell into the feeling of love and was ready to confess courageously to "You," which he thought was a friend, add to the charm of Seventeen, who returned to the form of a deeper man.
