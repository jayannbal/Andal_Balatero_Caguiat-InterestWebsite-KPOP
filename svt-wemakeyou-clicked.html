Introducing the album

We Make You is the debut Japanese mini album by SEVENTEEN. It was released on May 30, 2018 with "Call Call Call!" serving as the title track.

The song "CALL CALL CALL!" by SEVENTEEN spreads the message of being there for someone in need. The lyrics reinforce the idea that no matter how difficult the situation is, the band members will always be there for their loved ones, ready to lend a helping hand.

The verse emphasizes the togetherness and support that the band members have for each other. The pre-chorus talks about the stress and pressure one may feel in life, but also assures that someone will be there to listen and support them. The chorus urges the listener to call and reach for help and reinforces that they will always be there to run to the aid of their loved ones. The post-chorus serves as an encouraging melody to reach out and connect with others.
