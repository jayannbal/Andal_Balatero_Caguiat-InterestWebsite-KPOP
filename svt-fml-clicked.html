Introducing the album

"FML" is a newly coined English word, short for "F*ck My Life," a slang term that laments or blames oneself for one's life when bad things happen or when things go badly.

Rather than being buried in endless negative thoughts in a bad situation, Seventeen understands the conflict and wandering of youth from a healthy and positive perspective, saying, "Let's fight together instead of being pessimistic and frustrated," and presents a message of comfort and hope to them.

The album includes a total of six songs, including double title songs "F*ck My Life" and "Son Goku," as well as unit songs "Fire" (hip-hop team), "I Don't Understand But I Luv U" (performance team), "Munji" (vocal team) and group songs "April Shower." In particular, S.Coups, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Mingyu, Vernon, and Dino, led by album producer Woozi, will participate in writing or composing to present high-quality music.
