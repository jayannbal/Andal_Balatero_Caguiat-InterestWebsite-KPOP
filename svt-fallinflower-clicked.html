Introducing the album

"Fallin' Flower" or "Maiochiruhanabira (舞い落ちる花びら)" is the second Japanese single by SEVENTEEN. It was released on April 1, 2020, with "Fallin' Flower" serving as the title track.

The song "舞い落ちる花びら (Fallin’ Flower / Maiochiruhanabira)" by SEVENTEEN (세븐틴) explores themes of longing, growth, and finding meaning in life. The lyrics paint a picture of falling flower petals that no one reaches out to catch, symbolizing a sense of loneliness and detachment. The cold smiles and sadness mixed within the fallen petals represent the emotions people often hide behind a façade.

Overall, "舞い落ちる花びら (Fallin’ Flower / Maiochiruhanabira)" explores the journey of personal growth, finding meaning in relationships, and cherishing the present moment. It encapsulates the longing to meet someone special and the transformative power of love.
